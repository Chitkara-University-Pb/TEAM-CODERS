About Us

We plan on providing a user interface on a website from where the customers can order fresh organic fruits and vegetables directly to their house at a price almost same as the market.

we intend to make a place where you can register for the delivery of farm products at a regular interval chosen by you. For example if you choose the span of 3 days and save the farm products which you want to order in a cart, the items will be delivered to your doorstep. This can help to provide the customers as well as the local marketers and farmers to increase their sale.

Along with it we have an idea to decrease the wastage of fruits and vegetables. For example: if someone wants to buy a product at a short notice and in a small amount, the vendors or farmers will provide the products which are good and cannot be sold as consumers refuse to buy it. by this method the seller can sell and it can also be consumed fully with minimal wastage.
